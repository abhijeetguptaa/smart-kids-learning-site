Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Smart Kids Learning Math Games (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application. Our app is designed especially for children and families and complies with Google Play Families Policy and the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from children or users.

  • No name, email address, or phone number is collected.
  • No precise location data is collected.
  • No contacts, photos, or personal files are accessed.

2. Advertising

Our app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These ads are configured to be:

  • Child-directed and non-personalized
  • Compliant with Google Play Families Policy
  • Rated for general audiences (G-rated content only)

AdMob may collect limited anonymous technical data such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Basic interaction data for ad performance

This data is used only to provide safe, non-personalized ads and cannot be used to identify a child.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying child-safe advertisements

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children’s Privacy

We strictly follow COPPA and Google Play Families requirements. Since we do not collect personal information, parental consent is not required.

5. Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or stored, there is no risk of personal data misuse from our application.
